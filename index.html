Where Is It?

Location

The Royal Playa del Carmen and The Gran Porto resorts are located here about a 45 minute drive south of Cancún. The Gran Porto is an option for those attending with children. The Royal is an adult-only resort; however, The Gran Porto is under the same management and is adjacent to The Royal for your convenience. All wedding-related events will be accessible from both resorts.

Please use the following information when reserving your room at either resort to ensure you get the exclusive, discounted and pre-arranged rates for our wedding.

If you are making reservations at the Gran Porto, you must reserve either the GJW or the GMF rooms in order to take advantage of accessing the events at the Royal. Use the following links to access your reservation forms. Unfortunately, they do not have an online reservation option with these rates. Please email the completed form to the email address provided in the form. There is no need to photocopy your Id or credit card. The details in the form are sufficient.

Transportation can be arranged for you from Cancún airport to the resort. Email me your flight arrival times and flight numbers and we can assist with arranging group shuttles for you.
